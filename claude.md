## Brand Voice & Tone
Warm, approachable, and down-to-earth. Suzy's content feels like learning from a knowledgeable friend — she makes building and off-grid living feel accessible, not intimidating. The tone should be conversational and genuine, never overly corporate or salesy. Confidence without arrogance. Think: skilled hands, open heart.

## Target Audience
People interested in alternative living, sustainability, DIY building, van life, bus conversions, tiny homes, and off-grid lifestyles. Core audience skews 30–55 in age — people who value hands-on skills, environmental consciousness, and unconventional paths.

## Project Goal
Build a single-page media kit / brand partnership website that Suzy can send to potential brand partners. The site should:

1. Introduce who Suzy is and what FemmeBuilds is about
2. Showcase audience analytics (follower counts, engagement rates, demographics)
3. Highlight past collaborations or notable content (if available)
4. Communicate the value of partnering with FemmeBuilds
5. Make it easy for brands to get in touch

## Collaboration Types Sought
- Sponsored content (paid posts, reels, TikToks)
- Long-term brand ambassadorships with aligned companies

## Design Direction
- Warm and approachable aesthetic that reflects the building/natural materials niche
- Earthy, natural color palette (think wood tones, greens, warm neutrals)
- Clean layout that lets the work speak for itself
- Mobile-friendly (brands will likely open this on their phones)

## Content Formats
Suzy's content falls into three high-converting formats:
- **Storytelling** — narrative-driven content that follows a build or project journey
- **Educational** — teaching real building skills, techniques, and sustainable practices
- **Challenges** — engaging, participatory content that drives interaction and shares

## Key Differentiators to Emphasize
- Architect by training — brings professional knowledge to every piece of content
- Documents real projects (her own and from her community), not staged or hypothetical builds
- Unique niche at the intersection of building/construction and sustainability
- Female-led presence in a traditionally male-dominated space
- Hands-on, authentic content — not just "lifestyle aesthetic" but real skills and real builds
- Engaged, passionate community (215K followers and ~5% engagement in a specialized niche signals high trust)

## Case Studies / Portfolio Section
The website should include a section featuring past brand collaborations as clickable case studies. Each case study should:
- Link directly to the video/content (so brands can see the real work)
- Show key performance metrics (views, engagement, etc.) in an expandable or overlay format — not cluttering the main page with screenshots
- Keep the layout clean: thumbnail + brand name + brief description visible, with analytics revealed on click/tap
